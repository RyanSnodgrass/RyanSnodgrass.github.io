RUBY ON RAILS

From databases and servers, to stylish CSS - I am engineering solutions running on the popular and powerful Ruby on Rails platform following the “Rails Way”: convention over configuration, “DRY” coding, and RESTful routing.

SINATRA

About

Ryan is a late 20 something and a movie buff who recently moved to the Atlanta area from Orlando, FL. After realizing his degree in Cinematic Studies from University of Central Florida wouldn't land him his dream job, Ryan started learning to code at Tree House. He's been hooked ever since. He recently graduated from The Iron Yard in Atlanta after building more apps than he can count and earning even more comisssions.
